Our Mission
What We're Here To Do

We are established as an Installation, Maintenance and Training service company in Algeria. Our traditional business model is based on the safe accomplishment of services in the oil and gas markets in Algeria and around the world. We build strong, ongoing relationships with our customers, focused on satisfying their implicit and explicit needs.

🔭
Our Vision
Where We're Headed

To provide quality services that exceed the expectations of our esteemed customers. We will strive to attract, develop, and retain the best people and to keep them safe. By defying the risks and competition of the market and undertaking new projects in compliance with international standards, MGI Energy is shaping Algeria's industrial future.

Our Journey

From Idea to Industry Leader

A company built milestone by milestone, always grounded in technical excellence and client trust.

2018 — The Beginning
MGI Energy Founded

MGI Energy Group is incorporated as a private Algerian company. Founded with a clear mission: deliver world-class industrial maintenance to Algeria's energy sector with zero compromise on safety or quality.

2018
2019
2019 — First Contracts
Oil & Gas Entry

Secured first major contracts in the Algerian oil & gas sector, delivering valve repair and calibration services. Established our Laghouat workshop as the core operations hub in southern Algeria.

2020 — Expansion
Mobile Workshop Deployed

Launched our 20ft truck-mounted valve testing workshop — enabling us to bring full testing and repair capabilities directly to client sites across Algeria, drastically cutting downtime.

2020
2022
2022 — Regional Presence
Libya Branch Opens

Opened our Tripoli branch office, expanding operations into Libya. MGI Energy now serves clients across two countries, with teams fluent in regional industrial regulations and standards.

2024 — Portfolio Growth
Tier-1 Client Portfolio

Built a loyal roster of industry-leading clients including Sonatrach, Sonelgaz, GE Algeria Turbines, FieldCore, and CCL Global — all trusting MGI for precision maintenance on their critical assets.

2024
NOW
2026 — Today
Growing Stronger

With 250+ employees, 6 specialist services, and operations across Algeria and Libya, MGI Energy continues to expand — always with the same founding principle: Our Goal, Your Trust.
