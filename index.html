What We Do

Industrial Services Built for Demanding Environments

From factory-trained valve repair to turbulent oil flushing — our accredited technicians deliver precision maintenance that keeps your facility at full capacity.

01
🔧
Valve Repair Workshop

Factory-trained, fully accredited technicians repairing all makes and models of valves, control valves, and safety/pressure relief valves.

02
🚛
Valve Testing & Inspection

20ft truck-mounted testing workshop for shut-off and control valves — deployed directly to your site, minimizing operational downtime.

03
⚙️
Field & Workshop Calibration

Complete calibration solutions for commissioning, rotating spares, and ergonomics — available in-field or at our workshop facilities.

04
🌡️
Heat Exchanger Repair

Extracting, cleaning, camera inspection and full repair services for heat exchangers — restoring thermal efficiency and structural integrity.

05
💧
Turbine Oil System Flushing

Hydrodynamic turbulent flushing with full-flow absolute filtration — eliminating contamination and protecting your turbine investment.

06
🔩
Industrial Bolting Services

Reliable technical bolting ensuring leak-free operations and maximum safety across your high-pressure installations and pipelines.
